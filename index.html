Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our Android app.

**Information We Collect**

  • Device information: This includes your device's model, operating system version, and unique identifiers.
  • App usage information: This includes information about how you use our app, such as the features you use and the screens you view.
  • Crash logs: If your app crashes, we collect information about the crash to help us fix it.

**How We Use Your Information**

  • Improve our app: We use your app usage information and crash logs to improve the stability and performance of our app.
  • Understand how you use our app: We use your app usage information to understand how people use our app so that we can make it better.

**How We Share Your Information**

  • Firebase Crashlytics: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect and analyze crash logs. Firebase Crashlytics is a service provided by Google.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect and analyze app usage information. Firebase Analytics is a service provided by Google.

**Security**

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at stefandekanski@gmail.com

**Note:** We do not offer any opt-out or data deletion options to our users.
